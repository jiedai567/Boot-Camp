Dice Game (Zhi ShaiZi YouXi)

Rules:(Gui Ze:)

  • When you click the Roll Dice! button, you will roll up to 10 dice, one at a time.
  • If (RuGuo) you roll a 1, game stops and all further rolls are forfeited.
  • If (RuGuo) you roll 2, 3, or 4, you win nothing but you get to roll again.
  • If (RuGuo)you roll a 5, you win 5 gold coins and you get to roll again.
  • If (RuGuo) you roll a 6, you win 6 gold coins and you get to roll again.
